How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your Where To account and all associated personal data at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — In the App

Open Where To → go to the Profile tab → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email Request

Send an email to hello@wheretotrips.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your Where To account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email when complete.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email)
  • Saved destinations and travel preferences
  • Wander Together trip history, votes, and chat messages
  • Passenger details saved for booking (name, DOB, passport information)
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

What May Be Retained

  • Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data (not linked to your account)
  • Booking records required by law or for fraud prevention (if applicable)
