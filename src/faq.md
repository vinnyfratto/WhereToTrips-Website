      - q: "How are recommendations generated?"
        a: "WhereTo's AI Discovery Engine starts from your budget and your chosen vibe, then weighs real factors — flight prices from your home airport, stay availability, seasonality and weather, events, and how far your total budget stretches — to rank destinations that fit. The personalized matching runs inside the app."
      - q: "Where does booking actually happen?"
        a: "In the WhereTo app — never on this website. The site is for discovery and learning how WhereTo works. Personalized recommendations, flights, hotels, and activities are all handled in the iOS and Android app."
      - q: "Is the app free? How does WhereTo make money?"
        a: "WhereTo is free to download. We earn commissions from booking partners when you book travel through the app, so the discovery experience stays free to you."
      - q: "What does early access mean, and when does the app open?"
        a: "Before public launch, you can join the Early Access list to be among the first invited when the app opens. Join with your email and we'll notify you the moment it's available."
      - q: "Can I cancel or change a booking?"
        a: "Bookings made in the app follow the cancellation and change policies of the airline, hotel, or provider you book with. You'll see the specific terms before you confirm, and you manage changes from within the app."
      - q: "How does Wander Together handle group privacy?"
        a: "Each member of a group trip sets their own budget and preferences privately. WhereTo uses them to find destinations that work for the whole group — the group sees shared options and votes, not each other's private budget details."
      - q: "How do you keep budgets and prices accurate?"
        a: "Pricing shown in the app reflects live data from our travel partners at the time you search. Because availability and fares change constantly, the lists on this website are illustrative — your real, current matches always come from the app."
      - q: "What devices does WhereTo support?"
        a: "WhereTo is a mobile app for iOS and Android. The website works on any browser, but all planning and booking happen in the app."
