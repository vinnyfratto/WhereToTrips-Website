This policy explains how the WhereTo **website** (wheretotrips.com) handles information you provide here. It is separate from the **WhereTo app**, which has its own privacy policy covering accounts, travel preferences, and booking data.

## Information this website collects

The website is informational only. We collect information only when you choose to submit it through a form on this site:

- **Email address** — when you join the Early Access list or request the app download link.
- **Contact details and message** — your name, email, optional company, and message when you use a contact, creator, partner, or press form.
- **Travel context you volunteer** — for example, a departure city or interests mentioned in a message.

We do not sell this information.

## How we use it

We use the information you submit to:

- respond to your inquiry or route it to the right team;
- send the app download link or early-access updates you requested;
- improve the website and our outreach.

## Service providers

Form submissions on this site are processed by **Web3Forms**, which delivers them to our team. Email you provide may be handled by our email tools to send the messages you asked for. The site is hosted on **GitHub Pages**. These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the service.

## Your choices

Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe option, and you can ask us to delete the information you submitted at any time by contacting us.

## Relationship to the WhereTo app

Accounts, travel preferences, and any data collected during booking are governed by the **WhereTo app privacy policy**, not this one. See the [WhereTo app privacy policy](/privacy/) for details on data collected in the app and during booking.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the website evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

## Contact

Questions about this website privacy policy? Reach us through our [contact page](/contact/).
